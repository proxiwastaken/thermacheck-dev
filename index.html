Our Building Energy Compliance Services

Complete range of energy assessment and compliance services for your project

SAP Assessments

Standard Assessment Procedure calculations for new builds, extensions, and conversions to demonstrate Building Regulations compliance.

  • Design stage SAP calculations
  • As-built SAP calculations
  • Extensions & conversions

Energy Performance Certificates

On Construction EPCs for new build dwellings, lodged to the national register upon completion. Required for Building Control sign-off.

  • New build EPCs
  • Conversion EPCs
  • Lodged to national register
  • Fast turnaround

U-Value & Psi Calculations

Detailed thermal calculations for building elements and junctions. Essential for demonstrating fabric performance and achieving compliance.

  • U-value calculations per element
  • Psi-value (thermal bridging) calculations
  • Improved SAP performance
  • Detailed calculation reports

Highly Glazed Extensions

Specialist assessment for extensions where glazing exceeds 25% of floor area. Demonstrates compliance through whole-dwelling SAP calculation.

  • Whole-dwelling assessment

  • Clear compliance pathway

What We Need From You

SAP assessments are a desktop exercise - there's no need for a site visit. Simply email us your drawings and specifications.

To provide an accurate assessment and avoid delays, please supply as much of the following as possible:

Basic Project Information

  • Full property address (with postcode confirmed via Royal Mail)
  • Site plan at 1:500 showing orientation of the dwelling(s)

Drawings

  • Scaled floor plans of each storey (1:50)
  • Elevation drawings for each elevation
  • Sectional drawings of the dwelling (1:50)

Construction Details

  • External wall construction (including insulation type and thickness)
  • Roof construction (including insulation type and thickness)
  • Floor construction (including insulation type and thickness)
  • Window and door schedule (sizes, frame type, glazing type, U-values if known)

Heating & Hot Water

  • Make and model of boiler or heat pump
  • Hot water cylinder size (if applicable)
  • Heating emitters (e.g. radiators, underfloor heating)
  • System controls (programmer, room thermostat, TRVs)

Ventilation

  • Ventilation strategy (natural, MEV, MVHR)
  • Extractor fan details

Renewables (if applicable)

  • Solar PV details
  • Solar thermal details
  • Heat pump specifications
  • Other renewable technologies

Don't have all this information yet? No problem. If you're at early design stage, we can advise on minimum specifications to achieve compliance - that's what our advisory service is for.
